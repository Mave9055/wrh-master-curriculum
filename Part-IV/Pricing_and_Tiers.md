The **"What Really Happened" (WRH) Master Curriculum** is a comprehensive, professional, and non-clinical psychoeducational training package meticulously designed for federal and institutional procurement. This curriculum is specifically tailored to meet the rigorous requirements of agencies such as the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Department of Defense (DoD), Department of Labor (DOL), and the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA), providing a structured and replicable solution for behavioral health, recovery, and risk mitigation, with a critical emphasis on supporting the veteran population.

## **Tier Breakdown & Deliverables**

### **1. Foundation Tier (Estimated FFP: $18,500)**
*   **Performance Objective**: To establish foundational understanding in early-stage recovery and trauma-informed onboarding for participants.
*   **Scope**: Encompasses Sessions 1–26 (Part I: The System Logic Map).
*   **Key Deliverables**:
    *   **Facilitator Scripts**: Comprehensive, standardized scripts for 26 sessions, ensuring consistent program delivery.
    *   **Participant Workbooks**: Digital format, aligned with Part I content, for participant engagement and documentation.
    *   **Regulation Grounding Protocol (RGP) Training**: Foundation-level instruction in RGP, enhancing participant self-regulation capabilities.
    *   **Presentation Slides**: Access to all presentation materials for Part I, supporting visual learning and facilitator instruction.

### **2. Core Tier (Estimated FFP: $32,000)**
*   **Performance Objective**: To facilitate long-term behavioral health and comprehensive recovery journeys through advanced operational strategies.
*   **Scope**: Encompasses Sessions 1–56 (Part I & Part II: The Core Operational Journey).
*   **Key Deliverables**:
    *   **Facilitator Scripts**: Comprehensive, standardized scripts for 56 sessions, ensuring consistent program delivery.
    *   **Participant Workbooks**: Digital format, aligned with Part I & II content, for participant engagement and documentation.
    *   **Advanced RGP Training**: Advanced instruction in RGP, including detailed mechanism breakdowns, for enhanced self-regulation and operational understanding.
    *   **Pilot Program Slide Deck**: Complete presentation materials for the Pilot Program, supporting visual learning and facilitator instruction.

### **3. Master Tier (Estimated FFP: $48,000)**
*   **Performance Objective**: To provide institutional-grade risk mitigation capabilities and ensure professional facilitator standardization across the entire curriculum.
*   **Scope**: Encompasses Sessions 1–76 (Full Curriculum + Special Operational Addendum).
*   **Key Deliverables**:
    *   **Master Curriculum Access**: Full access to the 76-Session Master Curriculum, including all proprietary content.
    *   **Facilitator Standardization Toolkit**: Comprehensive toolkit (Part IV) designed to ensure consistent and high-fidelity program delivery by facilitators.
    *   **Crisis Package**: Federally Compliant Version 7.0 of Session 76, addressing crisis intervention and risk mitigation protocols.
    *   **Comprehensive Resources**: All presentation slides and master glossaries for the entire curriculum.
    *   **Institutional Usage Rights**: Full legal usage rights for the acquiring institution, enabling internal deployment and utilization of the curriculum.
