## **Tier Breakdown & Deliverables**

### **1. Foundation Tier ($18,500)**
*   **Target**: Early-stage recovery and trauma-informed onboarding.
*   **Sessions**: 1–26 (Part I: The System Logic Map).
*   **Deliverables**:
    *   Full facilitator scripts for 26 sessions.
    *   Digital participant workbooks (Part I).
    *   Foundation-level RGP (Regulation Grounding Protocol) training.
    *   Access to the **Part I Map** presentation slides.

### **2. Core Tier ($32,000)**
*   **Target**: Long-term behavioral health and comprehensive recovery journeys.
*   **Sessions**: 1–56 (Part I & Part II: The Core Operational Journey).
*   **Deliverables**:
    *   Full facilitator scripts for 56 sessions.
    *   Digital participant workbooks (Part I & II).
    *   Advanced RGP training and mechanism breakdowns.
    *   Complete **Pilot Program** slide deck.

### **3. Master Tier ($48,000)**
*   **Target**: Institutional-grade risk mitigation and professional facilitator standardization.
*   **Sessions**: 1–76 (Full Curriculum + Special Operational Addendum).
*   **Deliverables**:
    *   **Full 76-Session Master Curriculum**.
    *   **Facilitator Standardization Toolkit** (Part IV).
    *   **Session 76: Crisis Package** (Federally Compliant Version 7.0).
    *   All presentation slides and master glossaries.
    *   Full legal usage rights for the institution.
