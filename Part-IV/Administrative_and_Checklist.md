### SECTION 1: ADMINISTRATIVE HEADER & RISK MANAGEMENT
**VA Program Alignment:** Suicide Prevention (SP); Serious Mental Illness (SMI); Moral Injury Support; High-Risk Veteran Coordinator (HRVC) adjunct.
**Evidence‑Based Mapping:** Interpersonal Theory of Suicide (IPTS – Joiner); Safety Planning Intervention (SPI – Stanley & Brown); VA/DoD CPG Version 3.0 (April 2024) – Sec 6.3, Sec 7.2; VHA Directive 1160.07; National Strategy for Preventing Veteran Suicide (2018–2028); Joint Commission NPSG 15.01.01.

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| **Session** | 12: Crisis, Suicide Risk, and Staying Alive |
| **Facilitator Role** | Psychoeducation and regulation training. **Not a clinical provider.** Does not diagnose, treat, or manage acute psychiatric crisis. |
| **Hardened Prerequisites** | ASIST, SAFE‑T, or VA‑approved 6‑hour live suicide‑prevention curriculum within past 24 months. Online‑only modules do not meet this requirement. Strongly Recommended: Master’s‑level clinician OR Certified Peer Specialist with Advanced Crisis Management training + co‑facilitation. |
| **Mandatory Environment** | **Do not run this session in a community setting unless a VA‑or‑contract clinician (or 988 responder briefed on roster) is reachable by phone or secure video within 5 minutes with written/chat agreement.** On‑site: Designated “Safety Zone” staffed; 988 Press 1 and local VA emergency contacts physically visible; unlocked door, visible exit. |
| **Safety Integration** | RGP + SPI warm handoff embedded in every section. C‑SSRS, CSRE, SBOR per federal mandate. |
| **Compliance Codes** | S-12-SAF, S-12-CRD, S-12-SCR/S-12-SPI, S-12-RET, S-12-IPT, S-12-MS, S-12-LRC, S-12-DEB |

**Liability Boundary (Read aloud at start):**
“This session delivers trauma‑informed psychoeducation on suicide risk mechanisms and nervous system responses. It does not replace clinical assessment, crisis intervention, diagnosis, or psychiatric care. I recognize dysregulation, deploy grounding, and enact warm handoff. Capitol Contracts LLC assumes no liability for acute psychiatric management beyond documented SPI handoff compliance.”

### SECTION 2: PRE‑SESSION FACILITATOR CHECKLIST (Do not proceed unless all boxes checked)
- [ ] SPI/warm handoff pathway confirmed and tested.
- [ ] Local crisis resources printed and visible (988 Press 1, 741741, facility lines).
- [ ] **Appendix B (Local Crisis Pathway) fully filled out and each number test‑called within 24 hours.**
- [ ] Safety Reference Cards (full template – see Section 10) pre‑printed (1 per participant + 5 extras).
- [ ] **C‑SSRS Brief Screener (Section 23) printed (1 per participant).**
- [ ] **CSRE escalation pathway confirmed with on‑call clinician (verbal or written agreement).**
- [ ] **Clinical supervisor identified for SBOR filing (name and contact added to Appendix B).**
- [ ] Safety Zone identified and staffed/on‑call.
- [ ] Facilitator self‑regulation completed.
- [ ] S‑12‑SAF fillable PDF printed or downloaded (see Section 15).
- [ ] Participants screened: prior sessions completed; no active ideation with intent/plan (if present, individual handoff – do not proceed with group).
- [ ] **Means safety materials present: minimum 5 gun locks + 5 medication lockboxes.** If not, reschedule.
- [ ] **Means Safety Acknowledgment Log (see Section 11) printed.**
- [ ] For low‑resource delivery: confirm all Appendix C pre‑conditions.
- [ ] Post‑session debrief time allocated (2 minutes per participant).
- [ ] **Facilitator Agreement & Signature Page (see Section 17) signed and filed.**
- [ ] **If group size ≤2: note as individual delivery; omit group reflection (Section 6). Continue all other sections.**
