The **What Really Happened (WRH)** 76-Session Master Curriculum is a professional, institutional-grade training program. To ensure the safety and efficacy of the delivery, facilitators must meet the following certification standards.

## **Hardened Prerequisites**
Facilitators must possess one of the following qualifications within the past 24 months to deliver the **Session 76: Crisis Package**:
*   **ASIST** (Applied Suicide Intervention Skills Training)
*   **SAFE-T** (Suicide Assessment Five-step Evaluation and Triage)
*   **VA-Approved 6-Hour Live Suicide-Prevention Curriculum**
*   *Online-only modules do not meet this requirement.*

### **Strongly Recommended Qualifications**
*   Master’s-level clinician (LCSW, LPC, LMFT, PsyD, PhD).
*   Certified Peer Specialist with Advanced Crisis Management training.
*   Co-facilitation with a clinical supervisor for the first three cohorts.

---

## **WRH Certification Levels**

| Level | Sessions | Requirements | Delivery Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Foundation Facilitator** | 1–26 | WRH Level 1 Training (16 hours) | Certified to deliver Part I. |
| **Core Facilitator** | 1–56 | WRH Level 2 Training (40 hours) | Certified to deliver Part I & II. |
| **Master Facilitator** | 1–76 | WRH Master Training (80 hours) + Hardened Prerequisites | Certified for the Full Curriculum & Crisis Package. |

---

## **Maintaining Certification**
To maintain **WRH Master Facilitator** status, the following annual requirements must be met:
1.  **Clinical Supervision**: At least 10 hours of documented clinical supervision or peer review.
2.  **RGP Verification**: Demonstrated proficiency in the **Regulation Grounding Protocol (RGP)**.
3.  **Refresher Training**: Completion of the annual **WRH System Logic Update** (4 hours).
4.  **SPI Compliance**: Verification of the **SPI (Safety Planning Intervention)** warm handoff pathway.

### **Recertification Process**
Facilitators must apply for recertification every **24 months**. Failure to meet these standards will result in the suspension of the license to deliver the **Session 76: Crisis Package**.

---

## **Institutional Certification**
Institutions (VA, DoD, SAMHSA) may apply for **WRH Certified Provider** status by certifying at least **three Master Facilitators** and establishing a documented **CSRE (Comprehensive Suicide Risk Evaluation)** escalation pathway.
