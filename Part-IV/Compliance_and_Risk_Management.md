## **Overview**
The **"What Really Happened" (WRH) Master Curriculum** is engineered to meet and exceed the rigorous compliance standards required for federal contracting, specifically within the **Department of Veterans Affairs (VA)** and **Department of Defense (DoD)**. ## **2.0 Federal Compliance Standards**
Capitol Contracts LLC ensures that all curriculum delivery aligns with the following federal mandates:

### **2.1 VHA Directive 1030 Compliance**
The WRH curriculum integrates the **Three Lines of Model (3LM)** for risk management, ensuring oversight at the facilitator, institutional, and federal levels.
*   **Line 1**: Facilitator self-audit and real-time regulation.
*   **Line 2**: Institutional oversight and compliance monitoring.
*   **Line 3**: Independent audit and federal reporting.

### **2.2 SAMHSA Trauma-Informed Care (TIC) Standards**
Every session is audited against the six key principles of the SAMHSA TIC framework:
1.  Safety
2.  Trustworthiness and Transparency
3.  Peer Support
4.  Collaboration and Mutuality
5.  Empowerment, Voice, and Choice
6.  Cultural, Historical, and Gender Issues
