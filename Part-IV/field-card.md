# 📇 Facilitator Field Card: Safety & Fidelity Protocol

**Purpose:** To provide Peer Facilitators with a rapid-response framework for maintaining the "Educational Bridge" and ensuring participant safety without crossing into clinical therapy.

---

## 🚦 The Signal System

### 🟢 Green: Operational Flow
* **Status:** Participant is engaged, sharing within the scope of the session, and respecting group boundaries.
* **Action:** Continue with the curriculum script. Focus on active listening and peer validation.

### 🟡 Yellow: The Drift (Caution)
* **Status:** Participant begins "Trauma Dumping" (intense, unregulated detail), monopolizing time, or showing signs of moderate emotional distress.
* **Action:** **Soft Redirect.** * *Script:* "I hear the weight of that, and it’s a vital part of the story, but to honor everyone’s time and the session's goal, let’s bring it back to [Current Topic]. Let’s chat for a minute after the session."
* **Protocol:** Document the drift in the after-action report.

### 🔴 Red: Emergency Handoff (Stop)
* **Status:** Ideation of self-harm, threats of violence, or a total psychological "shut down" (dissociation).
* **Action:** **Immediate Transition.**
    * *Script:* "I’m going to pause the group for a five-minute break. [Name], let's step outside with [Co-Facilitator/Staff] to make sure you're supported right now."
* **Protocol:** Follow the pre-arranged **Clinical Warm Handoff** to the on-call specialist or VA crisis line.

---

## 🛡 Drift Correction Protocol
* **Stay in the "What":** If the conversation moves into "Why did this happen to me?" (Therapy), pull it back to "What do we do now?" (Skills/Action).
* **The Peer Barrier:** Remind the group: *"I am a guide on this path because I've walked it, not a doctor. We are here to build the gear, not perform the surgery."*

---

## 📋 Post-Session Checklist
1.  **Attendance Log:** Updated in the portal.
2.  **Safety Check:** Did anyone end in a "Yellow" state?
3.  **Fidelity Check:** Did we cover the core "Outlaw Gospel" principle for this session?
